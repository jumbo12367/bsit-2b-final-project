Welcome to BSIT 2-B ARCADE GAMES!

Our Story

BSIT 2-B ARCADE GAMES is a passion project developed by Krista Gronifillo, one of the developers from BSIT 2-B, a team of innovative students who are driven by their love for video games and game development. Combining retro gameplay with modern-day development techniques, Krista and the BSIT 2-B team have created a unique compilation that captures the heart and soul of arcade classics while offering an enhanced experience for today’s players.

Our Mission

At BSIT 2-B ARCADE GAMES, our mission is simple: to preserve the magic of arcade games and share that magic with a new generation of gamers. We’re dedicated to crafting experiences that spark the same excitement and joy that the original arcade machines brought to us. Whether you're racing against the clock, battling enemies, or jumping through challenging levels, every game in this compilation is designed to bring you back to the pure fun of arcade gaming.

What’s Inside

  • Snake: The classic game where you control a growing snake, trying to eat food while avoiding collisions with the walls. Simple yet addictively fun!
  • Tic Tac Toe: The iconic paper-and-pencil game reimagined for the screen! Challenge your friends in this timeless strategy game.
  • Mario: Dive into a platforming adventure with Mario, as you jump through levels, avoid obstacles, and defeat enemies in this nod to the legendary franchise.
  • Pac-Man: Guide Pac-Man through a maze, gobbling up pellets while avoiding the pesky ghosts. A true arcade classic that never gets old!
  • Tetris: The world-famous puzzle game where you fit falling blocks together to clear lines. It’s a game of strategy, timing, and quick thinking!
  • Asteroids: Hop into a spaceship and blast your way through fields of asteroids. Avoid collisions and keep your ship in one piece as you destroy obstacles and rack up points!

BSIT 2-B ARCADE GAMES brings you back to the heart of arcade gaming with updated controls and a smooth, modern experience.

The Team

The BSIT 2-B development team, a group of passionate and talented students, contributed their skills in game design, coding, and art to bring this compilation to life. Their dedication and collaborative spirit made this project a reality, and they continue to innovate in the world of game development.

Join the Fun!

Dive into BSIT 2-B ARCADE GAMES and relive the magic of arcade classics with a modern twist. Challenge yourself and most importantly—have fun! Whether you're in it for the high scores or the nostalgia, there’s a game for everyone.
